How To Use

  • Apply a nickel sized amount of Pure Flaxseed Gel. Finger comb your curls to distribute the gel and allow your hair to clump and curl properly. Avoid combs and brushes during this stage.

  • HANDS OFF: Don’t disturbb the defined curls while they dry.
